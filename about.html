Hi, I’m Edson

University of Waterloo - Electrical Engineering - Class of 2028

I design and validate electronics across analog front-ends, embedded interfaces, and power systems. I’m working toward advanced hardware architecture and neurocomputing in upper years, and aim to pursue graduate studies at MIT or Caltech. My project experience spans PCB design, ASIC verification, and industrial automation with PLCs.

Tools I’m comfortable with include Altium Designer, MATLAB, and GTKWave, plus Python-based verification via cocotb. I enjoy building drones, music production, and tackling signal integrity and control problems.

  • Interests: RISC‑V, neurocomputing, hardware‑software co‑design
